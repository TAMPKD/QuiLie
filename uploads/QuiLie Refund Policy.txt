REFUND POLICY — QuiLie
Last updated: 5/27/2026
FREE TRIAL FIRST
Every new user gets a 3-day free trial before any charge. We strongly
encourage you to use the trial to decide if QuiLie is right for you. No payment
is taken during the trial.
REFUND WINDOW
Because a free trial is provided, refunds on paid subscriptions are limited.
A refund may be requested only if BOTH conditions are met:
  (a) the request is made within 48 hours of the charge, AND
  (b) the subscription has not been substantially used after the charge
   (for example, no more than [2] reflections, journal entries, or
   challenges completed in that period).
NON-REFUNDABLE CASES
We do not provide refunds for:
Requests made more than 48 hours after the charge.
Subscriptions that have been actively used beyond the limit above.
Renewal charges where you forgot to cancel before the renewal date
(you can cancel anytime to stop future renewals).
Partial/unused periods after cancellation (access continues to period end).
HOW TO REQUEST
Email hello@quilie.life from your account email within 48 hours, with your
account email and reason. We aim to respond within [5] business days.
PROCESSING
Approved refunds are issued to your original payment method via our payment
partner (Razorpay or Paddle). Timing depends on the partner and your bank.
REGIONAL RIGHTS
If your local law grants you mandatory refund/withdrawal rights (for example,
certain EU/UK consumer rights), those rights still apply where required.
